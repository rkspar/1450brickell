Building Facts

Rising 35-stories about the heart of the priceless Brickell Business District, 1450 BRICKELL, Miami's newest Class-A corporate tower, offers headquarter prominence with unparalleled water views and commuter convenience.

With 582,817 rentable square feet consisting of floor plates ranging from 23,912 rentable square feet to 26,411 rentable square feet. 1450 BRICKELL is designed to create the optimal efficiency in space planning.

It features high speed elevators, 2 parking spaces per 1,000 SF, concierge services, valet parking, and panoramic views of Biscayne Bay and Downtown.

Located in the least congested part of Brickell Avenue, 1450 BRICKELL will be the most desirable office address in Miami.

1450 Brickell has already been awarded its Certificate of Occupancy.
